Overview

All structures are handcrafted and biome-aware. Some contain traps, references and Create contraptions.

Windmill Variations

  • Windmill — Acacia
  • Windmill — Oak
  • Windmill — Birch
  • Windmill — Cherry
  • Windmill — Spruce

Halloween Set

  • Dark Spooky House — trap + loot
  • Graveyard — 4 themed graves (Herobrine, Far Lands, Legends, Story Mode); hidden loot
  • Spooky Farm — broken auto wheat farm, cobwebs, loot
  • Spooky House I — abandoned house
  • Spooky House II — another variant

Copper Update

  • Greenhouse — 3 floors: crops → flowers under dome
  • Storage Hall — spawners and highlighted chest
  • Lightning Rod Tower — tall copper tower, chest and decor

Nether Expansion

  • Nether Forpost — piglin mini-base in overworld
  • Warped Greenhouse — warped forest variant
  • Crimson Portal — activated portal with a secret

Other Structures

  • Tower — technomage tower with diverse loot
  • Miner Hut — tools and armor; azurine+calcite roof
  • Post Office — box unpacker mechanic; some boxes contain loot
  • Camp — lumberjack / explorer camp
